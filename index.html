What is Powerlifting

Powerlifting is a sport of competitive weightlifting, where athletes compete in a series of three lifts in an attempt to achieve their maximal weight. These lifts, commonly referred to as the 'Big Three', consist of the squat, the benchpress, and the deadlift. Competition is divided by gender and organized into various weight-classes, allowing everyone a fair chance at being competitive.

Powerlifting challenges many aspects of an atheles skill. Although many confuse it to simply be a contest of raw strength, it takes a lot of hard work to achieve success. Atheletes put in non-stop effort day in and day out to obtain those results on competition day. Powerlifting is simple to understand, fun to participate in, and a lot of hard work.
All powerlifting meets follow the same order operations. Athletes first compete in the squat, followed by the bench press, and finally the deadlift. Athletes are allowed 3 attempts per lift, increasing in weight after successful attempts, and repeating weights on failure. Depending on the competition, atheletes are then awarded based on strength in each individual lift, as well as total weight lifted.
In preparation for competiton, atheletes use a variety of different techniques to improve their strength. There are a variety of different lifts, routines, and diets available to increase results, many of which are being used by own club's members. In addition to these widely used methods, there are many people who enjoy creating their own plans and strategies. A lot of thinking goes in to finding the optimal training experience, as each athlete grows and reacts differently.

About Us

Whether you're interested in casual lifting or serious compeition, beginner or veteran, our club has a spot for you. As a family, we work to provide a healthy environment that encourages a growth in strength, a sense of teamwork, and a sense of competition at all levels. Recently founded in the Fall of 2015, we look forward to a long future and a growing community on campus.

Strength

One of the three pillars of our club is the pursuit of strength. We work towards not only an explosive strength of body, but an unbreakable strength of mind. Our club promotes a healthy lifestyle, encouraging its members to be active, healthy, and happy. Our club overcomes personal obstacles and mental barriers through rigourous training. This allows for a refined sense of determination, accomplishment, and an improved ability to work hard to overcome challenges.

Teamwork

Missouri S&T Powerlifting Club values teamwork above all. We believe that teamwork is a key to success, and we work hard to help our members achieve their maximum potential. We are always looking to expand, and are always excited to see new faces. We welcome all levels of experience, even if you've never stepped inside of a weight room before. In the gym, we like to provide a positive environment where everyone is learning and everyone is having fun. We work together as a team and have fun like we're a family.

Competiton

At the heart of it all, our club is driven by competition. Powerlifting is a competitive sport in which you get to prove to yourself that all of your hard work has gotten you to where you are now. As a team, we help each other to become the strongest we can be. Competing with each other as well as competing with yourself is what makes powerlifting fun. Our club will be providing in house competitions during the school year, and travelling to different nationally sponsored competitions as well.
